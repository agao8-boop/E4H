The Problem

2 billion people cook with dirty fuels. Millions more face grids that fail daily.

Renters and low-income households bear the heaviest energy burden. They pay the most, control the least, and suffer the worst consequences when power fails.

Unreliable Grids

The Renter Trap

How It Works

Plug in. Power up. Stay resilient.

Our modular system pairs high-efficiency appliances with smart LFP batteries. No wiring, no landlord approval, no grid dependency.

1

Plug Into Any Outlet

Every appliance works with standard household outlets. No electrician, no hardwiring, no modifications. Unbox, plug in, and start saving.

2

Smart Battery Charges Off-Peak

AI-powered energy management charges your batteries when electricity is cheapest or when the grid is stable, building a reserve for when you need it most.

3

Cook and Power Through Outages

When the grid drops, your system switches seamlessly to battery power. Cook a full meal, keep lights on, and stay connected for hours without interruption.
