Flexible Financing Options

We understand that different customers have different needs. Our flexible payment models make energy resilience accessible to everyone, from individual homeowners to large organizations.

Lease Model Popular

Spread the cost over time with our flexible leasing options. Perfect for customers who want immediate energy resilience without a large upfront investment.

How It Works:

  • No upfront purchase required
  • Monthly payment plans (12, 24, 36, 48 months)
  • Maintenance & support included
  • Option to purchase at end of term
  • Upgrade path to newer models
  • Tax benefits for businesses

Best For:

Homeowners, small businesses, and organizations that want predictable monthly costs and the flexibility to upgrade over time.

Pay-As-You-Go (PAYGo) Most Flexible

Pay for your energy storage based on actual usage and savings. This performance-based model aligns our success with yours, making energy resilience accessible to everyone.

How It Works:

  • Zero upfront cost
  • Pay based on energy savings
  • Built-in metering & monitoring
  • Remote management included
  • Cancel or own after minimum term
  • Scales with your usage

Best For:

Renters, income-qualified households, and customers who want to try before they buy with zero initial investment.

Bulk & Fleet Procurement

Organizations purchasing multiple units benefit from volume discounts, streamlined deployment, and centralized management tools.

How It Works:

  • Volume-based pricing tiers
  • Coordinated installation schedules
  • Fleet management dashboard
  • Aggregated demand response
  • Custom financing structures
  • Dedicated account management

Best For:

CCAs, housing authorities, property management companies, and municipalities deploying across multiple locations.

Power Purchase Agreement (PPA)

A third-party ownership model where we own and maintain the equipment while you purchase the energy services at a predetermined rate.

How It Works:

  • E4H owns & maintains equipment
  • Customer pays per kWh delivered
  • Fixed rate for contract term
  • 20-25 year agreements typical
  • All maintenance included
  • Performance guarantees

Best For:

Non-profit organizations, municipal facilities, and commercial properties seeking predictable energy costs without capital expenditure.

Community Solar + Storage Model

Combine community solar subscriptions with shared battery storage for maximum savings and resilience at the neighborhood level.

How It Works:

  • Shared solar + storage infrastructure
  • Virtual net metering credits
  • Proportional cost allocation
  • Community resilience hub option
  • Subscription-based access
  • Scalable for any community size

Best For:

Apartment complexes, homeowners associations, and communities where individual installations aren't feasible.
